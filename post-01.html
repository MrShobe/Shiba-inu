Among us is a game that you can play on mobile,pc,nintendo switch,X-box and Playstation.

Among us was made in 2018 by innersloth (the same company that made the henry stickmin collection) but got REALLY famous around 2020.

The game is about 1 to 3 imposters hiding among crewmates sabotaging their ship and killing them. the goal of the imposter is to stay hidden and kill all the crewmates though the crewmates win if they finish all their tasks or vote the imposter out of the ship.

Over the years among us got less popular but people still use among us memes and even if no onee plays among us anymore it will still be the best game of 2020.
